04 — Framework

The Innovation
Storytelling Framework

A structured process for converting technical complexity into stories that move people to action.

Step 01
Understand the Innovation
Identify the technology, its function, and its core value proposition. Understand what makes it genuinely different.
Step 02
Define the Audience
Segment users, consumers, investors, policymakers, or global markets. Know who you're speaking to before you speak.
Step 03
Translate the Complexity
Convert technical language into simple, human-centered messaging. Make the unfamiliar feel intuitive.
Step 04
Build the Narrative
Create a story that explains the problem, solution, impact, and value. Give audiences a reason to care.
Step 05
Localize for Market Adoption
Adapt messaging for cultural, industry, and global market contexts. Universal ideas need local voices.
Step 06
Measure Communication Impact
Track adoption, engagement, trust, visibility, and conversion outcomes. Stories succeed when they drive change.
